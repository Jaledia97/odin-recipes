Lasagna

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Beef & sausage
  • Mozzerella
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pasta sauce

Step-By-Step

  1. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into a 9" x 13" pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  2. Top the noodles with some of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  3. Cover with foil and bake. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan, and bake for another 15 minutes.
